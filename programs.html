How to Choose the Best Program

The best program is not always the hardest one. A successful training split should match your schedule, support recovery, and give you enough structure to progress over time.

At Antons Hybrid Way, the focus is on selecting a system that fits your lifestyle instead of forcing a routine that cannot be sustained.
